2023 IC3 Personal Data Breaches FBI – Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3) The 2023 IC3 report documented over 55,000 complaints involving personal data breaches. Cybercriminals often gain unauthorized access to networks and sensitive data, which can then be used for identity theft or financial fraud. Data breaches are a key method used for larger cybercrime schemes, highlighting the need for stronger security, multi-factor authentication, and user awareness.

CyberScoop – 2024 IC3 Key Stats CyberScoop The 2024 IC3 summary shows cyber-enabled fraud caused 83% of losses, totaling $13.7B. Older adults (60+) filed the most complaints and suffered the greatest financial harm. Hacking-related incidents are rising, exposing personal data and compromising accounts. These trends highlight the sophistication of cybercriminals and the need for better cybersecurity across all age groups.
